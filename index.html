About Us

Our Story

Helping Hands was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to bring hope and help to underserved communities. What began as a small group of passionate individuals has grown into an international organization with projects in over 20 countries.

We believe that sustainable change comes from empowering local communities. Our approach focuses on education, healthcare access, and economic development programs designed to create long-term solutions.

Our Values

  • Compassion for all people
  • Transparency in our operations
  • Sustainable solutions
  • Community partnership
  • Accountability for results
